  1. 1 recipe empanada dough(what is that :?)
  2. 8 ounces whole milk(uh oh!!!) mozzarella cheese
  3. 4 ounces chedder cheese
  4. 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  5. 1/2 cup pizza sauce, OR marinara sauce, PLUS!!! extra for dipping, but onli if you want X)
  6. 4 ounces pepperonis
  7. 1 BIG egg

okay those are the ingredients, time to make the empanada

  1. prepare the empanada dough..... cover it with plastic wrap and let it rest for 10 minutes
  2. grate the mozzarella and chedder and place them into a medium bowl, then add half of the parmesan cheese AND half a cup of the marinara sauce and then mix it good
  3. preheat the oven to 400 degrees farenheight and divide the dough into 20 small pieces (like 50 g each) and gently roll each into a little ball :)
